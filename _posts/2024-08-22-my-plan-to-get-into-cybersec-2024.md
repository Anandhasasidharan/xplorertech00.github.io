<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP): A more advanced certification for those with experience.<br />-Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH):Focuses on penetration testing and understanding how hackers think. Online Courses & Bootcamps<br />-Cybersecurity Bootcamps: Intensive programs that offer hands-on experience and practical skills.
